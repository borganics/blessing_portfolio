About Me

From the Lab to Your Practice

I have stood in a medical laboratory collecting patient samples and running tests. I know what it feels like to work in a fast-paced clinical environment where accuracy matters and there is no room for error.

That experience shaped me. And now I am bringing it into a new chapter as a Medical Virtual Assistant.

I help healthcare professionals like doctors, clinic owners, nurses, and telehealth providers reclaim their time by handling the admin work that pulls them away from patient care.

Unlike most VAs, I do not just understand the paperwork. I understand the work behind it. The labs. The patients. The pressure.

If you are a healthcare provider drowning in admin, let us talk. Send me a DM with the word "MEDICAL" and let us get you back to your patients.
